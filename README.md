# 🐾 HuskyBuddy

> *Connecting Huskies, one coffee chat at a time.*

HuskyBuddy is a data-driven matchmaking platform built exclusively for Northeastern University students. By pairing Huskies based on shared interests, majors, career goals, and more, HuskyBuddy makes it easy to meet new people, have meaningful conversations, and grow your network.

---

## Project Status

**~30% Complete** — Active development in progress.

---

## The Problem

Northeastern is a large, fast-paced campus. Between co-ops, study abroads, and packed schedules, it's easy for students to feel disconnected. Existing solutions fall short:

- **YikYak** — Anonymous, impersonal, and connections rarely leave the app.
- **LinkedIn** — Too formal; not built for casual, genuine conversation.
- **GroupMe** — Group chats are overwhelming, and unverified accounts make it hard to know who you're talking to.

HuskyBuddy fills this gap with a **verified, personalized, and low-pressure** way to meet fellow Huskies.

---

## Features

- **Smart Matching** — Pairs students every two weeks based on major, year, interests, career goals, favorite campus spots, and availability.
- **Verified Huskies Only** — Every account is tied to a Northeastern student, ensuring a safe and trusted community.
- **Icebreaker Games** — After matching, an introductory game kicks off the conversation so neither student feels the pressure of a cold open.
- **HuskyBuddy Gallery** — After completing a coffee chat, students upload a photo to their profile, building their network and credibility over time.
---

## Profile Fields

When signing up, students fill out:

- Name & Northeastern email
- Major(s)
- Year
- Interests & hobbies
- Favorite spots around campus
- Past internship / co-op experiences
- Current student status *(on-campus, co-op, study abroad)*
- Availability
